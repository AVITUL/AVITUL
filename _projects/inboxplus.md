The application uses predefined patterns to match important texts from a clutter of received messages. Presents the different types of messages with extracted information, like banking messages are presented in a ledger view. Bookmyshow messages are presented in a Movie card view. IRCTC messages in a Travel card view.

A home page provides "today's" overview of messages, presents user with reminders. Custom reminders are also included.

Associated with National Institute of Technology Kurukshetra.
