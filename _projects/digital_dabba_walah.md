To digitize the Mumbai dabbawalah experience for TCS employees. The project supports booking a slot, tracking driver's location, and status of lunch pack. It also enables drivers to manage pickups.

Project was developed in Visual sutdio, as a Hybrid application. Backend was written is C#.
