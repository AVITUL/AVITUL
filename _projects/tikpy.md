TikTok has no public API. TikPy is a lightweight API, without the requirement of any browser sessions, to collect the public data available at https://www.tiktok.com. Collect trending posts, video metadata, user metadata, hashtags, music, related hashtags, related music and, download videos with the API. Everything is returned in a JSON object.

Associated with Precog @ IIITD.
